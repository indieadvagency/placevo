Frequently asked questions

How do I plan a trip?

Open the Trips tab → Plan a new trip, describe your destination, and Placevo builds an itinerary for you. You can also add places from the Map or Places tabs.

How do I split a bill?

Open the Receipt Calculator, add the people and items (or scan a receipt), set tax/tip/delivery, and tap Calculate split. It handles quantities and even a free/birthday meal.

How do I import places from TikTok or Instagram?

Go to the Import tab, paste a link or the caption, and Placevo extracts the places for you.

How do I report or block someone?

Open a post or a user's profile and use the Report or Block option. Blocked users can no longer interact with you, and reported content is reviewed.

How do I reset my password?

On the email sign-in screen, use "Forgot password" to receive a reset link, or sign in with Google.

How do I delete my account?

Email oussamasallam@gmail.com from your account address and we'll delete your account and data within 30 days.
